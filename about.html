S A N A M

ABOUT

Sanam is a prominent Indian pop-rock band known for its refreshing take on classic Bollywood songs and original compositions. Formed in 2010, the band consists of four members: Sanam Puri (lead vocals), Samar Puri (lead guitar), Venky S (bass guitar), and Keshav Dhanraj (drums). Early Days and Rise to Fame The band initially called themselves "SQS Project" and started gaining popularity through their YouTube channel. They uploaded covers of well-loved Bollywood songs, which resonated with a broad audience. Their unique blend of contemporary pop-rock with traditional Indian melodies, along with Sanam Puri's soulful voice, helped them stand out in the crowded music scene.

Musical Style Sanam's music style is a fusion of pop, rock, and Indian classical music. They are well-known for their innovative renditions of classic Bollywood hits, breathing new life into songs from past decades.
